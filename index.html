SKN Interior Solutions is a premier, full-service firm redefining luxury across Delhi/NCR. We are masters of both commercial and residential design with over 30+ years of experience in the industry, executing complex projects with uncompromising precision and artistic flair.

From Vision to Reality: The SKN Interior Solutions Legacy

  • Founded in 2019, SKN Interior Solutions was established to bring a new level of professionalism and technical mastery to the interior design and construction industry. While the SKN name is modern, our foundation is built on over 30 years of elite industry experience under the leadership of our Managing Director.
  • We have spent decades managing high-stakes, large-scale commercial projects, but we have translated that same level of corporate discipline and structural precision into the residential sector.
  • Commercial Powerhouse. Residential Experts. We do not just decorate homes; we engineer them. We understand that a luxury residence requires the same technical rigor as a commercial complex and a commercial space needs the same aesthetic soul as a home. Whether it is a high-functioning corporate office or a bespoke premium villa, we bring a vast depth of knowledge to every square foot.
  • Our Comprehensive 360 Degree Ecosystem. We are one of the few firms capable of handling a project from the first brick to the final light fixture. By offering an end-to-end Turnkey Solution, we eliminate the stress of managing multiple vendors.

  • Complete Interior Design: Bespoke finishes that reflect your brand or your personal lifestyle.
  • MEP and HVAC Excellence: Professional-grade Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing services, alongside specialized HVAC systems for climate-perfect environments.
  • Design-to-Delivery: A seamless transition from blueprints to a fully functional, move-in-ready space.
